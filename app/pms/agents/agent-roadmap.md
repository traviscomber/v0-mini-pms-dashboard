# Agentic PMS Roadmap

## North Star
Build a hotel operations system where the product does not just display data; it interprets it, prioritizes it, and turns it into action.

## Phase 1: Executive Intelligence
- Ship a single Chief of Staff agent.
- Add executive briefing, risk scoring, and priority ranking.
- Keep the experience read-only first so confidence is high.

## Phase 2: Revenue Lift
- Add a Revenue Strategist with pricing, forecast, and upsell recommendations.
- Connect reservation context, occupancy, and seasonal signals.
- Measure ADR uplift, conversion rate, and revenue at risk recovered.

## Phase 3: Operational Control
- Add an Operations Commander for housekeeping, arrivals, departures, and task flow.
- Auto-rank critical work by impact and SLA.
- Measure room turnaround time, late-clean rate, and task completion.

## Phase 4: Guest Experience Automation
- Add a Guest Concierge for pre-arrival, in-stay, and post-stay communication.
- Personalize by language, profile, and stay context.
- Measure response time, check-in completion, and upsell conversion.

## Phase 5: Trust and Scale
- Add an Integrations Engineer and a Trust Auditor.
- Introduce evals, traces, approvals, and failure-aware execution.
- Measure sync health, incident rate, and time-to-root-cause.

## Launch Rules
- Use a reasoning model for planning and escalation.
- Use a fast GPT model for focused, well-defined execution.
- Never let an agent act without a visible audit trail.
- Prefer human approval when money, guest experience, or compliance can be affected.
