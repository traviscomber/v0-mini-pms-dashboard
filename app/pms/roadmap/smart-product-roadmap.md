# Smart Product Roadmap

## Goal
Make the PMS feel like an intelligent operating system, not a data browser.

## Phase 1 — Decision Layer
- Show the top 3 next best actions.
- Route the user to the right module with one click.
- Explain why each action matters and how much it matters.

## Phase 2 — Specialized Agents
- Revenue Strategist: pricing, forecast, and revenue protection.
- Operations Commander: room readiness, staffing, and task flow.
- Guest Concierge: pre-arrival and service recovery messaging.
- Trust Auditor: traceability, approvals, and safety checks.

## Phase 3 — Execution Loop
- Let the operator approve actions.
- Convert approvals into tasks, messages, or reservations workflow updates.
- Track what was suggested, approved, and completed.

## Phase 4 — Learning System
- Learn patterns by property and season.
- Surface recurring issues before they repeat.
- Personalize recommendations by role and property type.

## Phase 5 — Boardroom Experience
- Keep one clear view for executives.
- Keep operational detail one click away.
- Make every screen answer: what changed, what matters, what now?
