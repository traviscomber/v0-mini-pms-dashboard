# N3uralia Premium PMS GUI - Complete Design Guide

## Overview
Successfully redesigned the lightweight PMS with a **premium N3uralia dark-mode enterprise aesthetic** inspired by enterprise SaaS dashboards. Dark background with purple/teal accents, gradient highlights, and polished interactions.

---

## Design System

### Color Palette (OKLCH)
- **Primary**: `oklch(0.5 0.18 270)` - Deep Purple
- **Secondary**: `oklch(0.45 0.15 160)` - Teal 
- **Accent**: `oklch(0.55 0.2 280)` - Vibrant Purple
- **Background**: `oklch(0.08 0 0)` - Deep Dark
- **Card**: `oklch(0.12 0.01 270)` - Dark with purple tint
- **Foreground**: `oklch(0.95 0.002 0)` - Crisp white
- **Destructive**: `oklch(0.65 0.2 20)` - Red for alerts
- **Border**: `oklch(0.2 0.02 0)` - Subtle dark
